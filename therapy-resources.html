Juniper Tree Psychotherapy

Over the past 15 years, JuniperTree has built a reputation for individualized, effective psychotherapeutic treatment. With a broad experience base, our team is well positioned to help navigate whatever you’re facing. By working together, we can build a collaborative, positive plan for healing and growth. Our therapists are well versed in a variety of intervention strategies to help transform your current situation. Looking for a psychotherapist in the Newmarket area? With priorities of making therapy approachable and un-intimidating, we help to establish your goals, and provide guidance and interventions along the way. Whether it’s navigating neurodivergence, managing strong emotions, healing hurt or trauma, or something else entirely our team can support you on your path. You can contact JuniperTree at https://junipertree.ca/

Rock of Peace Counselling

Hello from Rock of Peace Counselling! We specialize in helping individuals with ADHD, ASD and/or LD’s overcome the unique challenges encountered in daily life. Through evidence-based and personalized approaches, we partner with you to enhance your day-to-day functioning, manage symptoms, and develop the skills you need for long-term success. For example, if you struggle with ADHD, together, we foster a deeper understanding of how ADHD affects you individually while identifying your strengths and capabilities. Our goal is to improve focus, enhance organizational skills, and manage impulsivity by helping you implement practical strategies that lead to meaningful and lasting change. For individuals with ASD, we provide compassionate and collaborative therapy that emphasizes clear communication, structure, and consistent support. Our therapists work to understand your sensory sensitivities, social challenges, cognitive processing style, and strengths. We aim to help you build emotional regulation and social interaction skills, empowering you to navigate societal and workplace environments with confidence. (Sliding scale fees may apply.) Go to rockofpeace.com for further information, or Call us at 416-802-4648.

Wild Sage Therapy

At Wild Sage Therapy, we specialize in providing modern, online counselling for Millennial & Gen Z teens/adults (16+) across Ontario, who are wanting to feel confident, reduce stress, and have healthy, fulfilling relationships. Our team focuses on a range of challenges including: living with ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder & Learning Disabilities, Anxiety, Depression, Trauma, Relationships & Dating, Big Life Changes, Stress & Burnout, Self-Esteem & more. We give you tools & strategies to support you in the present, while also helping you dig deeper, make connections you might not see yourself, and break patterns that are holding you back. We’re also Millennials & Gen Zs ourselves and get that life can be confusing, overwhelming, and straight-up exhausting these days. We want you to know that you don’t have to go through it alone and that feeling good about yourself, having direction, and living the life you want is totally possible. We also offer 24/7 online booking so you can fit appointments into your busy schedule. Visit our website to learn more or to get in touch: www.wildsagetherapy.ca We can’t wait to hear from you! Samantha Mirarchi & the Wild Sage Therapy Team

Blossom Lives

At Blossom Lives, we provide comprehensive psychotherapy, family support, and specialized training to help individuals, couples, families, and organizations find the right environment for them to blossom and grow emotionally, cognitively and emotionally. We are dedicated to supporting neurodivergent individuals and their families, offering therapy, parenting guidance, advocacy support, and resources to navigate school systems, healthcare, and government services. Beyond therapy, we provide workshops, courses, and training for parents, educators, and professionals, equipping them with practical tools to foster resilience, communication, and emotional regulation. Our corporate wellness programs focus on mental well-being, stress management, and team dynamics, helping workplaces create supportive environments. We also collaborate with healthcare providers, schools, and community organizations to provide holistic, integrative care. Whether you’re seeking personal support, parenting strategies, or professional development, Blossom Lives is here to help you grow, heal, and flourish. No referral is needed—contact us at grow@blossomlives.org or visit www.blossomlives.org to learn more.

Inner Peace Therapy

At My Inner Peace Therapy, we are committed to providing personalized and compassionate care to support each client’s unique journey. Within our diverse team, we have therapists who are experienced in working with individuals diagnosed with ADHD, OCD, learning disabilities, and autism. These therapists tailor their approach to address the specific needs and goals of each client, including support around emotional regulation, school-related challenges, and social or behavioral concerns. We strive to create a safe and inclusive space where clients can feel understood, empowered, and supported. We offer in-person sessions in Newmarket and Barrie, as well as virtual and phone sessions, depending on the therapist. To connect with us, feel free to email info@myinnerpeacetherapy.ca, call or text 289-763-9898, or visit our website at www.myinnerpeacetherapy.ca

We provide psychological, psycho-educational, psycho-vocational assessments and diagnosis for older teens, young adults, and older adults.
