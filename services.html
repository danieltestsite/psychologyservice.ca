Services

Services Offered

We provide psychological, psycho-educational, psycho-vocational assessments and diagnosis for older teens, young adults, and older adults.

Assessment/Diagnosis
We provide psychological, psycho-educational, psycho-vocational assessments and diagnosis for older teens, young adults, and older adults. Our assessments are comprehensive, and can also be specified to focus on particular issues, such as Autism Spectrum Disorder, Learning Disabilities, Dyslexia, Executive Function/ADHD, or Depression, Anxiety, Vocational, and Psycho-social issues.
Counselling

We can provide counselling for previously diagnosed/assessed conditions. Our areas of focus in counselling are disabilities, depression, ASD(Asperger’s), ADHD/Executive Function, anxiety, and social skills.

Groups

We also provide group sessions in social skills training/counselling using the PEERS for Teens and PEERS for Young Adults programs to assist those requiring communication/social interaction assistance.

ADHD Coaching
We have a Learning Strategist and Assistive Technologist on call who is available to provide coaching support to individuals requiring executive functioning/ADHD assistance in academics or in personal life.
Social Skills Training

Social interaction knowledge and abilities that allow individuals to successfully and productively interact with other individuals in their family, community, at school, and at work. It is about knowing the unwritten 'rules' of communication and social interaction and applying to every situation.

Consultations

Not sure what you need? Schedule a consultation with us and we might be able to help you find the service or assessment that works for you.

Why Choose Us

We provide you with a wide range of expertise in the diagnosis and assessment of ASD, ADHD/Executive Function issues, LD, Memory, and associated psychosocial/psychiatric issues. We believe that good diagnosis and assessment leads to good treatment. We can help you to identify what the issues are that you are confronting and help you plan how to deal with/treat those issues or obtain appropriate supports. If you are experiencing difficulty with postsecondary, workplace, ‘female presentation of ASD’, social interaction/communication, or issues affecting individuals facing retirement (who experience any of the foregoing), we have the experience to diagnose and help you find the services, treatment or supports that you may need.
